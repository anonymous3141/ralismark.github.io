--- layout: post title: Timeshare tags: interactive ---

Timeweave wasn't working for me, so I made an alternative. You can add people's timetables by using their webcal link. All of your data is store client-side only; there's no server at all!

Even though it's quite usable at this stage, Timeshare is still a prototype that I hacked together one day - there probably are still some bugs and missing features. For one, this relies on whateverorigin.org.
