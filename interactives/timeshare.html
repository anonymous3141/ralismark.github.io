Timeshare

A working client-side-only alternative to timeweave

  • 23 February 2020
  • 1 min to read

Timeweave wasn't working for me, so I made an alternative. You can add people's timetables by using their webcal link. All of your data is store client-side only; there's no server at all!

Even though it's quite usable at this stage, Timeshare is still a prototype that I hacked together one day - there probably are still some bugs and missing features.
